Volume Control: By pinching your thumb and index finger on your left hand, the program will control the system volume.
Brightness Control: By pinching your thumb and index finger on your right hand, the program will control the screen brightness.Explanation of Key Sections
MediaPipe and OpenCV Initialization: This sets up the hand-tracking model with confidence thresholds.
Volume and Brightness Control:
Left hand controls the volume using the distance between the thumb and index finger.
Right hand controls brightness in a similar way.
Output:
Volume and brightness levels are displayed in real-time, with colored indicators and percentage values.
